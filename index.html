Welcome to Study Spot Finder

Study Spot Finder is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that helps UMT students locate and review study areas on campus. Whether you're looking for strong Wi-Fi, comfort, or peacefulness, this app helps you decide and contribute by adding your own feedback.

  • 📍 Locate study areas on an interactive map
  • 📸 Upload photos and write your reviews
  • 💬 Explore what others say about each spot
  • 📦 Use offline after loading once (PWA)
